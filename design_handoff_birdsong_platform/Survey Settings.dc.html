Audience & goal

Edit

Parks & rec directors approaching CivicRec renewal. Understand what's driving them to look at alternatives and qualify who's ready for a sales conversation. Interview closes at 25 responses or Aug 15, whichever comes first.

Questions

Edit

The interviewer asks up to 2 follow-ups per question, in your brand voice.

01 How does your department handle program registration today?
02 What prompted you to look at alternatives this year?
03 Walk me through the last registration season that really tested your team.
04 Who else is involved in the renewal decision, and what do they care about?
05 What would need to be true for switching to feel worth the disruption?

Qualification

Edit

A respondent is marked qualified when they confirm renewal within 12 months, name a budget owner, and describe an active pain with their current tool.
